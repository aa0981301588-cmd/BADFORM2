01_概念

The technological life has become an irreversible pathological experiment. Our physiological structures are forced to adapt to the constraints of screens, interfaces, and artificial environments. This is not natural evolution; it is a rapid, induced deformation.

We document these anomalies not as diseases, but as the inevitable next stage of human development. Welcome to the archive of the future bodies.

科技生活已成為一場不可逆的病理學實驗。我們的生理結構被迫適應螢幕、介面與人造環境的限制。這並非自然的演化，而是一場迅速且被誘導的形變。

我們記錄這些異常狀況，並非將其視為疾病，而是人類發展必然的下一個階段。歡迎來到未來身體的檔案庫。
